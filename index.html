How to Fold

  1. Fold the paper in half lengthwise, then unfold.
  2. Fold it in half widthwise, then unfold.
  3. Fold each short side into the center crease, then unfold.
  4. Fold widthwise again and cut the center crease only halfway.
  5. Open it, fold lengthwise, push the ends together, and collapse into a booklet.
